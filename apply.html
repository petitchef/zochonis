How to Apply

The Zochonis Charitable Trust is only able to consider applications from charities registered with the Charity Comission. Applications should be in writing and may be submitted either by email or by post. If you wish to be considered for funding please write to us on your charity's letterhead giving a brief description of your charity.

Please include a 'Plan on a Page', detailing your current funding requirement, how the money will be spent and the success criteria for which you are aiming. Please include a copy of your latest audited accounts. For your application to be considered a copy of your accounts must accompany your application.

Please send your application to:

The Trust will only consider funding for their current financial year. We do not accept applications for multi-year funding. You may only submit one application in any 12 month period. If you have any queries regarding your application please contact marie.gallagher@zochonischaritabletrust.com
