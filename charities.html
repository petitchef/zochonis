Education

Each year the Trust donates a substantial portion of its income to schools, universities and learning institutions, which provide a wide range of wonderful educational opportunities for children of all ages.

ZCT funds secondary school bursaries making some of the best education in the Greater Manchester area accessible to bright children from low-income families.

In addition, the Trust supports a wide range of artistic, historical and scientific endeavours through outreach programmes to provide forward thinking and innovative learning experiences for young people. These culturally enriching programmes foster creativity, insight and self-confidence.

Children & Young People

The Trust recognises that there are a number of issues facing young people today such as gaining employment, managing mental health challenges, body image anxieties and social media pressures. With this in mind, ZCT supports a number of charities whose work specifically focuses on helping young people meet such challenges and to fulfil their potential.

Social Equity

The Trust has consistently provided support to some of the most deprived and vulnerable people in Greater Manchester. The work of the charities that it supports varies enormously, ranging from the prevention of human trafficking to alleviating elements of poverty and homelessness.
